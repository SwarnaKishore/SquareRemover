Instructions to play

"Square Remover" is a single player game
During the game you will need to make exactly 250 moves.
Each move consists of choosing two tiles on the board that share a side and swapping them with each other.
At any point in the game, if there exists a 2x2 square on the board where all 4 tiles have the same color.
Your score is increased by 1 and 4 tiles of the square get replaced with other colors.
Your goal is to obtain the maximum possible score in the end of the game.
